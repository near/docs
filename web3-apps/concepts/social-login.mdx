---
title: Social Login
description: "Allow users to login with email or social accounts, automatically creating a NEAR wallet for them"
---

Social login lets users access your dApp without needing prior knowledge of crypto wallets. Upon login, a NEAR wallet is automatically created for the user, which they can fund and use to interact with your dApp.

The recommended approach is [Privy](https://www.privy.io/), which provides an excellent developer experience for non-custodial social login.

---

## Privy (Recommended)

[Privy](https://www.privy.io/) is a third-party service that allows users to login using their email or social accounts (Google, Facebook, Twitter, etc). Upon login, a NEAR wallet is created for the user.

![Preview](https://framerusercontent.com/images/ugUCPrqIGlKFdxBwSbRoWriZtE.png?scale-down-to=2048&width=4018&height=2262)

<Tip>
Check our [Privy Integration Example](https://github.com/near-examples/hello-privy/) to learn how to integrate Privy into your web app.
</Tip>

**Key features:**
- **Non-custodial**: Users own their keys; Privy never has access to funds
- **Social providers**: Google, Twitter, Discord, GitHub, and more
- **Email & SMS**: Passwordless login via magic links or one-time codes
- **Embedded wallets**: Wallets are created automatically on first login
